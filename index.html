The daily consumption of coffee, a beloved ritual for many, carries both positive and negative aspects. On the positive side, coffee can boost alertness and productivity, providing a welcomed energy kick. Its antioxidant properties offer potential health benefits.

However, excessive intake may lead to insomnia, jitteriness, and increased heart rate. Dependency and withdrawal symptoms can also emerge. Furthermore, overuse may stain teeth and cause digestive discomfort. Moderation is key, as a balanced coffee habit can enhance focus and enjoyment while avoiding adverse effects on sleep and well-being.
